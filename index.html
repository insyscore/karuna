About karuna hospitals

41 Years of Excellence in Eye Care & Healthcare

For over four decades,our hospital has been at the forefront of providing exceptional healthcare. Specializing in Ophthalmology, Gynecology, Pediatrics, Nephrology, and Hemodialysis, we offer comprehensive medical care with a patient-centered approach. Our experienced team of specialists is committed to improving the quality of life for every patient through advanced treatment options and compassionate care.
